HR Support:
- Employees can contact HR through the app's Feedback or Questions section.
- For urgent matters, employees should directly email the HR team.

Technical Support:
- Issues with login, modules, or app access should be reported using the Feedback page.
- Employees may also restart the app or clear cache if issues continue.

Training Support:
- Employees should complete modules assigned to their role.
- If training content is unclear, they can report issues inside the modules.

Confidentiality Note:
- All employee questions are treated confidentially.
- The AI Assistant does not reveal information about other employees or internal decisions.
