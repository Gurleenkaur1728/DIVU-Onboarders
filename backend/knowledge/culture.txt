Our company values:

1. Teamwork  
   Employees are encouraged to collaborate, support each other, and communicate openly.

2. Integrity  
   We expect honesty and transparency in all professional interactions.

3. Innovation  
   Employees are encouraged to bring creative ideas and explore better ways of working.

4. Respect  
   Every employee is treated with fairness and dignity.

5. Continuous Learning  
   Training and development opportunities are available throughout the year.

Work Culture:
- We focus on creating a positive, inclusive work environment.
- Employees are encouraged to maintain work-life balance.
- New employees can ask questions freely—there are no “bad” questions here.
