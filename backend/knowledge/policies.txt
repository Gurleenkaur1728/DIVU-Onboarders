Leave Policy:
- Sick Leave: Employees are entitled to 5 paid sick leave days per year.
- Casual Leave: Employees receive 7 paid casual leave days annually.
- Emergency Leave: May be granted at the discretion of HR.
- Unpaid Leave: Must be approved by HR and direct manager.

Attendance Policy:
- Employees must clock-in using the attendance system.
- Repeated late attendance may require HR review.
- Remote workers must check in through the designated online portal.

Working Hours:
- Standard hours: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
- Break time: 1 hour.
- Flexible hours may be available depending on the role.

Confidentiality:
- Employees must not share internal company data.
- HR-related queries are handled privately and securely.

Code of Conduct:
- Employees are expected to behave respectfully and professionally at all times.
