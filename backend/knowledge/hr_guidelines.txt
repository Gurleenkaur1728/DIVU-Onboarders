HR Guidelines:

Communication:
- Employees should communicate respectfully in all HR interactions.
- Email responses may take 24–48 hours.

Document Verification:
- HR verifies uploaded documents within 3–5 business days.

Role Information:
- Role-specific responsibilities can be found in the Employment > Role Info section.

Feedback:
- Employees are encouraged to provide feedback about onboarding or training.

Probation Period:
- New employees typically have a 3-month probation period.
