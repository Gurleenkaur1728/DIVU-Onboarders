Q: What is the purpose of the onboarding program?
A: The onboarding program helps new employees understand company processes, tools, expectations, and culture.

Q: Where can employees submit required onboarding documents?
A: Employees can upload documents inside the Employment > Documents section of the app.

Q: What should I do if I cannot access a module?
A: Employees can contact HR or their manager. The DIVU app may also mark modules unavailable if they are not assigned.

Q: Who should I reach out to if I have HR-related questions?
A: Employees should contact the HR department using the Feedback or Questions section of the app.
