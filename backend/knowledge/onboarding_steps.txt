Onboarding Steps:

Step 1: Profile Setup
- Employees must update their basic details inside the Account > Profile section.

Step 2: Submit Required Documents
- Documents such as ID, bank details, and employment forms must be uploaded.

Step 3: Complete Assigned Modules
- Modules include company overview, workplace safety, culture, and job-specific training.

Step 4: Attend Orientation Events
- Employees must attend scheduled onboarding events.

Step 5: Ask Questions
- Employees can use the Questions section or AI Assistant for support.

Completion:
- Once all steps are complete, employees receive a digital certificate.

What are Modules?
Modules are interactive learning units in DIVU that help employees learn about:
- Company Overview: Mission, vision, values, and organizational structure
- Workplace Safety: Security protocols, emergency procedures, and safety guidelines
- Company Culture: Core values, work culture, team collaboration, and expectations
- Job-Specific Training: Role-specific skills, tools, and processes
- HR Policies: Leave policies, benefits, code of conduct, and compliance
- Technology & Tools: Software, systems, and platforms used in daily work

Each module contains:
- Learning content (text, images, videos)
- Interactive elements
- Quizzes to test understanding
- Progress tracking
- Completion certificates
