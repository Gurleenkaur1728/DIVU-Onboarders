Onboarding Steps:

Step 1: Profile Setup
- Employees must update their basic details inside the Account > Profile section.

Step 2: Submit Required Documents
- Documents such as ID, bank details, and employment forms must be uploaded.

Step 3: Complete Assigned Modules
- Modules include company overview, workplace safety, culture, and job-specific training.

Step 4: Attend Orientation Events
- Employees must attend scheduled onboarding events.

Step 5: Ask Questions
- Employees can use the Questions section or AI Assistant for support.

Completion:
- Once all steps are complete, employees receive a digital certificate.
